# Crystal Flower

Crystal flowers are small, glowing flowers that generate on grass throughout the surface of the overworld. They are a source of [magic crystals](/main/item/magic_crystal) which is particularly useful early in the game.

## Appearance

Crystal flowers are similar in size to most single-block flowers in vanilla *Minecraft*. They have a green stem, on top of which sit three colored petals which appear to have a crystalline shape. Crystal flowers produce randomly colored sparkle particles which float slowly upwards. They also emit a dim glow at night.

## Obtaining

Crystal flowers can be found growing in small clusters on grass fairly commonly throughout the overworld. They are perhaps easiest to find at night and in forested biomes, since their glow makes them easy to spot in dark areas.

## Usage

Currently, the only use for crystal flowers besides decoration is crafting them into magic crystals. Each crystal flower yields two magic crystals when placed in a crafting grid.
